Child Safety Standards

NearBudy is committed to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy towards any content or behavior that endangers minors.

Strict Prohibitions

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Exploitative or abusive behavior involving minors
  • Grooming, trafficking, or sexual exploitation
  • Any content that endangers children

Reporting & Enforcement

Users can report inappropriate behavior and content directly within the app. Reported content is reviewed promptly, and accounts violating our policies are removed and reported to relevant authorities where required.

NearBudy complies with applicable child safety laws and cooperates fully with law enforcement agencies to ensure a safe community for all.
